The Icecrown Gunship Battle " is an aerial battle between the gunships: Skybreaker (Alliance) and the Orgrim's Hammer (Horde) around the spire of Icecrown Citadel. The encounter has some fun elements and most guilds and PUG's will manage this fight quite easily as it is often refered to as the "Lootship Battle"

Abilities

All NPCs have the same abilities regardless whether they're Alliance or Horde, only their designations change.

Own Ship

Alliance Gunship Cannon/Horde Gunship Cannon

  • Cannon Blast - Launches a cannonball at the target. Inflicts 1000 Siege damage and generates 6 to 10 Heat.
  • Incinerating Blast - Consumes all of the cannon's Heat to launch a massive ball of fire at the target. Inflicts 1000 Siege damage plus additional damage per point of Heat

Muradin Bronzebeard/High Overlord Saurfang

  • Battle Fury - A stackable buff that increases all damage done by 5%. Instant. 20 sec duration. +7% in 25-player mode
  • Cleave - Inflicts 120% of normal melee damage to an enemy and its nearest allies, affecting up to 3 targets.
  • Rending Throw - The boss throws his weapon at an ally causing 3010 to 3990 weapon damage and an additional 2000 Physical damage every 3 sec for 18 sec. This debuff can stack infinatly.

Skybreaker Sorcerer/Kor'kron Battle-Mage

  • Below Zero - Freezes the target in a block of ice by concentrating and freezing moisture in the air. The caster must channel to maintain the ice block.

Skybreaker Mortar Soldier/ Kork'ron Rocketeer

  • Rocket Artillery - Launches a rocket that explodes on impact, inflicting Fire damage to nearby enemies and Siege damage to the enemy gunship.
    • Explosion - The rocket explodes, inflicting 6300 to 7700 Fire damage to nearby enemies and Siege damage to the enemy gunship.

Boarding Party

  • Bladestorm - Instantly Whirlwind up to 4 nearby targets and for the next 6 sec you will perform a whirlwind attack every 1.0 sec. While under the effects of Bladestorm, you can move but cannot perform any other abilities but you do not feel pity or remorse or fear and you cannot be stopped unless killed.
  • Wounding Strike - Inflicts 200% weapon damage and leaves the target wounded, reducing the effectiveness of any healing by 25% for 10 sec. -40% healing in 25-player mode.
  • Desperate Resolve - Increases armor and attack speed by 60%. +120% Armor in 25-player mode.

Skybreaker Marine/ Kor'kron Reaver

  • Desperate Resolve - Increases armor and attack speed by 60%. +120% Armor in 25-player mode.

Skybreaker Rifleman/Kor'kron Axethrower

  • Shoot - Shoot at the target, inflicting 3800 to 4200 Physical damage. 2 sec cast. 5700-6300 in 25-player mode.
  • Hurl Axe - Hurl an axe at the target, inflicting 3800 to 4200 Physical damage. 2 sec cast. 5700-6300 in 25-player mode

All enemy NPCs except the Commanders and the Battle-Mage/Sorceror gain experience the longer they are left alive, starting from Regular and progressing to Experienced, then Veteran, and finally Elite. Depending on rank, damage and attack speed are increased.
